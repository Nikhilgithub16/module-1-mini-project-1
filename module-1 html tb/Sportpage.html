Sports

Sports Officer: Mr. Pankaj Jain

Faculty Olympiad Conducted at LNCT Campus

The sports and leisure facilities on campus are :



Outdoor :Cricket, Hockey, Soccer, Volley Ball, Badminton, Tennis, Table Tennis, Softball, Squash etc.. These facilities are readily available to faculty and students for recreation and exercise. There are two play grounds of 2 acres and 2.5 acres each.

Indoor :There is also a 2000 sqft. Indoor sports complex facilities for like carom- board, chess, table- tennis.Three semi badminton courts and one indoor court are also available .

Students are encouraged to participate in national games and international games. Engineers' Olympic are being held since 2014. In Engineers' Olympic of 2016 seventeen games were included. Around one thousand five hundred students from various engineering colleges participated in it. LNCT felicitated Shri. Ashok Dyanchand on the eve of Engineers' Olympic
