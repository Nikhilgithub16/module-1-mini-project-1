FAQ Page

Question:

1. When does the admission start in LNCT University for PG and UG program and how does one apply?

The admission process for Diploma, UG and PG programs commences from 2022. Application form is available on payment of Rs.300. To apply one may:
Option 1: Apply online, Upload documents, Pay online
Option 2: Download Application Form for offline submission.
Option 3: Contact LNCTU Admission Cell at University Campus for Direct Admissions Process. For detailed information on the procedure to be followed for applying to any program in LNCTU, Bhopal, Click Here

2. What documents are required along with the Application form and within how many days a form gets processed?

Only a copy or scanned copy of class 10th mark-sheet and a recent color passport size photograph is required to be submitted/uploaded along with the Application Form. Confirmation about receipt of your form is to be checked on Microsite. It normally takes 6-7 working days to process the form.

3. What is Admission Microsite and how is the Registration/Interview/Selection status communicated to the candidates?

Admission Microsite is an online portal at LNCTU.ac.in. An applicant can check his Registration/ Interview/ Selection status by logging into his/ her LNCTU Admission Microsite with the application form number and the password at regular intervals for all admission related information. No separate communication is send in this regard.

4. What are the significance of Option 1 & Option 2 in the Application Form and can I change my program from Option 1 to Option 2 after registration of Application Form?

Students may apply for two programs under Option 1 & Option 2, e.g. MBA (Finance) and MBA (HR) respectively. Subject to meeting the eligibility criteria, you will be called for Selection process for Option 1. If you are called for Selection process for Option 2, it implies that you are not meeting the eligibility criteria of Option 1. Change is possible before selecting the Selection process date by sending mail request at admissions@LNCT.ac.in
